France: Macarons

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 3 large egg whites
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Food coloring (optional)
  • Buttercream or ganache for filling

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 300°F (150°C).
  2. In a bowl, sift together almond flour and powdered sugar.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk egg whites until foamy, then gradually add granulated sugar until stiff peaks form.
  4. Gently fold the almond mixture into the egg whites until well combined. If desired, add food coloring.
  5. Pipe small circles onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Let the piped macarons sit for 30-60 minutes until a skin forms.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes until firm and slightly risen. Allow to cool.
  8. Sandwich two macarons together with buttercream or ganache.

Singapore: Chendol

Ingredients

  • 1 cup green rice flour jelly
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup palm sugar syrup
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Prepare the green jelly using rice flour and pandan extract.
  2. In a bowl, combine coconut milk and palm sugar syrup.
  3. Serve the jelly over ice and pour the coconut mixture on top.

Malaysia: Kuih Lapis

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • Pandan juice for coloring

Instructions

  1. Mix rice flour, coconut milk, and sugar until smooth.
  2. Divide the batter and add pandan juice to one half for coloring.
  3. Layer the mixtures in a steaming tray and steam until cooked.

Sri Lanka: Kulikuli

Ingredients

  • 1 cup roasted gram flour
  • 1/2 cup jaggery
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

Instructions

  1. Melt jaggery with a little water and mix with gram flour and coconut.
  2. Add cardamom powder and mix well.
  3. Shape into small balls and let cool.

Oman: Halwa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon rosewater
  • Spices (cardamom, saffron)

Instructions

  1. Heat semolina in a pan until golden brown.
  2. Add sugar and water, stirring until thick.
  3. Add rosewater and spices, then cool and serve.

Dubai: Baklava

Ingredients

  • 1 package filo pastry
  • 2 cups chopped nuts (walnuts, pistachios)
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Instructions

  1. Layer filo pastry in a baking dish, brushing with butter.
  2. Add chopped nuts, then layer more filo, brushing with butter.
  3. Cut into diamond shapes before baking.
  4. Simmer honey, water, and sugar to make syrup, then pour over baked baklava.
